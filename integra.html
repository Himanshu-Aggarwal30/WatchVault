Xylys Integra Swiss Stainless Steel Black Dial Analog Watch for Men

₹30,000.00

Product Details


This watch from the Integra collection is powered by the Swiss-made Ronda Calibre 515 movement, known for its reliability. The 316L stainless steel case is accented with rose gold-plated elements and houses a midnight black dial with a Clou de Paris-inspired pattern and a date window at 6 O'clock. Rose gold-plated hands and indices, enhanced with Superluminova, offer excellent visibility. The integrated bracelet features multi-finished links with rose gold-plated accents, creating a perfect blend of sport and elegance. Sapphire glass with multiple layers of anti-reflective coating protects the dial, and the watch is water-resistant up to 5 ATM.

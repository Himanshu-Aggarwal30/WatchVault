Xylys Triumph Swiss Stainless Steel Blue Dial Chronograph Watch for Men

Rs. 32,500.00

Product Details


Powered by the Swiss Ronda Calibre 5040.D movement, this chronograph from the Triumph collection is both sophisticated and functional. The 316L stainless steel case, paired with a rose gold-plated bezel, houses a sunray blue dial with a guilloche-inspired central pattern and three sub-eyes. Rose gold-plated hands and indices, illuminated with Superluminova, ensure readability in low light. The bracelet features brushed stainless steel side links and rose gold-plated centre links for a refined finish. The sapphire crystal with anti-reflective coating offers protection, while the watch is water-resistant up to 5 ATM.

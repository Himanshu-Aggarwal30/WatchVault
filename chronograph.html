Product Details


The Xylys Grey Dial Chronograph watch for men is a luxurious Swiss-made timepiece designed with a strong carbon fibre material in the lower dial. Along with a Ronda 8040.N calibre chronograph movement, the watch is also water-resistant and can withstand up to 10 m underwater. It comes with Retrograde day indicator that precisely marks the day and month of the year. The strap is two-toned to add that refined touch, and the case is made from robust stainless steel. The lock mechanism of the watch is designed with the deployment buckle for easy fastening. The glass is crafted from the durable sapphire crystal which offers adequate protection against rough use. The elegant design combined with efficient functionality makes this a must-have timepiece in your collection.
